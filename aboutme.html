Overview

I was born in Hershey, Pennsylvania on the 28th of January, 2000. I grew up in the small and rather unknown town of Quarryville, near the southern end of Pennsylviana, in Lancaster county. I first touched a computer at around the age of 6 or 7, and have been interested in them ever since then. My interests have been varied over my life, and will continue to change in the many years forward.
